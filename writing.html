Novel: Heart's Door

Language: Chinese
Words: 39K
Brief Bio: Ashi, Axin, Anan, Xiaoyiu, and Qingqing are friends since high school, and Aze has a secret crush on Qingqing despite being 7 years younger than her. All six characters have secrets that they've never shared and keep hiding them behind their invisible "heart's door". In this novel, chapters are presented through each of these characters and depict their stories of love, friendship, and family.
